Abs Exercises

Abs exercises are designed to strengthen and define the core muscles, including the rectus abdominis, obliques, and transverse abdominis, improving posture, balance, and overall core stability.

Best Abs Exercises

Plank

The plank is a core-strengthening isometric exercise that engages the abs, back, and shoulders by holding a straight, rigid body position.

Russian Twists

Russian twists are a core exercise that targets the obliques and abdominal muscles by rotating the torso side to side while seated, often with added weight for increased intensity.

Leg Raises

Hanging leg raises are an advanced core exercise performed by hanging from a pull-up bar and lifting the legs toward the chest, effectively targeting the lower abs, hip flexors, and improving grip strength.
