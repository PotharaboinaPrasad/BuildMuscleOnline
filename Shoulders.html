Shoulder Exercises

For a well defined, round and absolute dense Shoulders.

Best Shoulder Exercises

Shoulder Press

The shoulder press is a strength training exercise that targets the deltoid muscles by pressing weights overhead to build shoulder size and upper body strength

Lateral Raises

Lateral raises are an isolation exercise that targets the side deltoid muscles by lifting weights out to the sides, helping to build wider and more defined shoulders.

Upright Rows

Upright rows are a compound exercise that targets the shoulders and upper traps by lifting weights vertically along the front of the body, promoting upper body strength and muscle definition.

Front Raises

Front raises are an isolation exercise that targets the front deltoid muscles by lifting weights straight in front of the body, helping to build shoulder strength and definition.

Face Pulls

Face pulls are a shoulder and upper back exercise that targets the rear deltoids and rotator cuff muscles by pulling a cable or band toward the face, improving posture and shoulder stability.

Dumbbell Shrugs

Shoulder dumbbell shrugs target the upper trapezius muscles by lifting the shoulders straight up while holding dumbbells, helping to build neck and upper shoulder strength and improve posture.
