Back Exercises

Strengthen and sculpt your back with targeted exercises for power and posture

Best Back Exercises

Pull Ups

Pull-ups are a powerful bodyweight exercise that builds upper-body strength and defines your back and arms.

T bar rows

T-Bar Rows target the middle back, building thickness and strength with controlled pulling motion.

Lat Pull down

The lat pulldown is a strength training exercise that targets the latissimus dorsi muscles in the back by pulling a weighted bar down toward the chest.

Deadlift

Deadlifts are a full-body powerhouse move that builds strength, stability, and muscle from head to toe.

Hip thrust

Hip thrusts isolate and strengthen the glutes, improving lower body power, posture, and athletic performance.
