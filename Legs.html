Leg Exercises

Leg exercises help strengthen and tone the lower body muscles, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves.

Best Leg Exercises

Leg Extensions

Leg extensions are an isolation exercise that targets the quadriceps by lifting a weighted pad using the lower legs.

Weighted Squats

Weighted squats are a compound exercise that builds strength in the legs, glutes, and core by squatting with added resistance like a barbell or dumbbells.

Leg Press Machine

The leg press is a powerful machine-based exercise that effectively targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes by allowing you to push heavy weights with your legs in a controlled, seated position, promoting lower body strength and muscle growth.

Hamstring curls

Hamstring curls are an isolation exercise performed on a machine that strengthens and tones the hamstring muscles by curling the lower legs toward the glutes against resistance, improving flexibility, stability, and lower body strength.

Leg Calves

Calf exercises focus on strengthening and toning the calf muscles—primarily the gastrocnemius and soleus—through movements like heel raises, helping improve lower leg power, balance, and ankle stability.
